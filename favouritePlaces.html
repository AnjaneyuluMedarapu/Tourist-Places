Favourite places

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a renowned white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It's a UNESCO World Heritage site and a symbol of love and architectural beauty.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is a sacred Sikh shrine located in Amritsar, India. Its distinctive golden architecture is a significant religious and cultural symbol for the Sikh community.

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace, located in Karnataka, India, is a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture. Also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, it served as the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty, the former royal family of Mysore. The palace is renowned for its grandeur and is a popular tourist attraction.
